  • Instructions for using the app

    • Use "a" button to move selected button and click. (was space bar but that screwed with the calculator?)

    • 1 "a" press > previous button
    • 2 "a" presses > next button
    • 3+ "a" press > click button

    "a" button presses must occur in within a 0.5 second interval.
